26-8-2018 Nashville Predators trade
Nashville Predators traded Kevin Fiala and Eeli Tolvanen to Florida Panthers for Evgeny Dadonov and Grigori Denisenko

A native of Russia, Evgeny Dadonov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 3rd round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 738 NHL games during his career, notching 189 goals and adding 335 assists along with 391 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.

A native of Russia, Grigori Denisenko was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 16 NHL games during his career, notching 4 goals and adding 2 assists along with 18 penalty minutes. The 18 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of Switzerland, Kevin Fiala was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 22 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of Finland, Eeli Tolvanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 154 NHL games during his career, notching 47 goals and adding 55 assists along with 74 penalty minutes. The 19 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.
