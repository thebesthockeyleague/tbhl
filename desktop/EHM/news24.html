23-8-2018 Brian Dumoulin frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Brian Dumoulin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brian Dumoulin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 2nd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 26 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
