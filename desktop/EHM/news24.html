3-9-2018 Patric Hornqvist frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins right wing Patric Hornqvist feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Patric Hornqvist was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 7th round of the 2005 entry draft. He has appeared in 509 NHL games during his career, notching 153 goals and adding 165 assists along with 247 penalty minutes. The 31 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
