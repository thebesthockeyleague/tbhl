2-8-2018 Alexander Radulov frustrated
Dallas Stars right wing Alexander Radulov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Alexander Radulov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 1st round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 154 NHL games during his career, notching 47 goals and adding 55 assists along with 74 penalty minutes. The 32 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
