21-8-2018 Andreas Johnson frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs right wing Andreas Johnson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Andreas Johnson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 7th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
