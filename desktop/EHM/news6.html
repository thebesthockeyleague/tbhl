24-8-2018 Brock McGinn frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes left wing Brock McGinn feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brock McGinn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 21 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 1 assists along with 10 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.
