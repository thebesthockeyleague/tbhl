24-8-2018 Trevor van Riemsdyk frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes defenseman Trevor van Riemsdyk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Trevor van Riemsdyk, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 80 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 47 assists along with 32 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.
