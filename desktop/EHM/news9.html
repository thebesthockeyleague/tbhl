21-8-2018 Elvis Merzlikins frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets goaltender Elvis Merzlikins feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Latvia, Elvis Merzlikins was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old goaltender is expecting a good season for the team.
21-6-2001 Dave King praises Ostap Safin
Columbus Blue Jackets head coach Dave King has praised young left wing Ostap Safin in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.
