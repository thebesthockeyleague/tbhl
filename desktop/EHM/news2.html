21-8-2018 Detroit Red Wings name captain.
Detroit Red Wings have named Niklas Kronwall as the captain of the team.
21-8-2018 Rocco Grimaldi frustrated
Detroit Red Wings center Rocco Grimaldi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Rocco Grimaldi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 25 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
21-8-2018 Darren Helm frustrated
Detroit Red Wings center Darren Helm feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Darren Helm was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 5th round of the 2005 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 31 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.
