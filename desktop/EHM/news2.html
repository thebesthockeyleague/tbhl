24-8-2018 Julius Vahatalo frustrated
Detroit Red Wings left wing Julius Vahatalo feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Julius Vahatalo was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 6th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 495 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 110 assists along with 469 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
