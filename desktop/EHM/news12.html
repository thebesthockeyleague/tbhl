21-8-2018 Nicolas Deslauriers frustrated
Edmonton Oilers left wing Nicolas Deslauriers feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nicolas Deslauriers was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 3rd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 169 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 16 assists along with 148 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
