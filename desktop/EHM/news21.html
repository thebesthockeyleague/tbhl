A native of Canada, Cam Talbot, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 31 year old goaltender is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of Canada, Ben Harpur was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
