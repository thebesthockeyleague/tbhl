A native of Canada, Patrick Eaves was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 545 NHL games during his career, notching 99 goals and adding 91 assists along with 182 penalty minutes. The 34 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
22-8-2018 Washington Capitals trade rumoured
Chicago Blackhawks are rumoured to be planning a trade sending defenseman Sami Niku and CHI 3rd round pick in 2019 draft to Washington Capitals for goaltender Vitek Vanecek

Rumours from various sources around the league say that the Chicago Blackhawks are looking for a backup goalie and that Vitek Vanecek would be a good fit there.
