30-8-2018 Brayden Schenn frustrated
St. Louis Blues center Brayden Schenn feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brayden Schenn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 354 NHL games during his career, notching 84 goals and adding 109 assists along with 179 penalty minutes. The 27 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.
