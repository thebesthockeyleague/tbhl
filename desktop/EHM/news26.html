21-8-2018 Jay Bouwmeester frustrated
St. Louis Blues defenseman Jay Bouwmeester feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jay Bouwmeester was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2002 entry draft. He has appeared in 88 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 3 assists along with 22 penalty minutes. The 34 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
