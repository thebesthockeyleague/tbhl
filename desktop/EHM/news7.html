21-8-2018 Ivan Nalimov frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks goaltender Ivan Nalimov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Ivan Nalimov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 6th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old goaltender is waiting for a good start this season.
