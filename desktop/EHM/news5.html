21-8-2018 Tim Harrison frustrated
Calgary Flames right wing Tim Harrison feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Tim Harrison was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 6th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
