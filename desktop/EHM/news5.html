A native of Canada, Anthony Peluso was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 6th round of the 2007 entry draft. 21-8-2018 Curtis Lazar frustrated
Calgary Flames center Curtis Lazar feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Curtis Lazar was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 4 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 1 assists along with 111 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
