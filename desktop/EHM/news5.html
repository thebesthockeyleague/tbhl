31-8-2018 Mikael Backlund frustrated
Calgary Flames center Mikael Backlund feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Mikael Backlund was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 380 NHL games during his career, notching 72 goals and adding 103 assists along with 143 penalty minutes. The 29 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.
