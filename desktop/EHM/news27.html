24-8-2018 Ryan McDonagh frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning defenseman Ryan McDonagh feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Ryan McDonagh was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 390 NHL games during his career, notching 43 goals and adding 127 assists along with 164 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.
