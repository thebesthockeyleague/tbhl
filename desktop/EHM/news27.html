24-8-2018 Alex Galchenyuk interviewed in paper
Alex Galchenyuk was interviewed in today's paper about the recent trade that sent him from Arizona Coyotes to Tampa Bay Lightning. To the fans and us players it's a game, but we can't forget it's also a business. Sometimes in business they have to make the tough decisions, I can't hold it against them, he told reporters.
24-8-2018 Tampa Bay Lightning trade
Tampa Bay Lightning traded Ryan Callahan, Anthony Cirelli, Callan Foote and TB's round 1 pick in 2019 to Arizona Coyotes for Alex Galchenyuk

A native of USA, Alex Galchenyuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of USA, Ryan Callahan was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 4th round of the 2004 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 33 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.

A native of Canada, Anthony Cirelli was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 21 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of Canada, Callan Foote was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 5 NHL games during his career, notching 7 goals and adding 0 assists along with 76 penalty minutes. The 19 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.

23-8-2018 Brandon Gormley frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning defenseman Brandon Gormley feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brandon Gormley was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 285 NHL games during his career, notching 20 goals and adding 50 assists along with 159 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
