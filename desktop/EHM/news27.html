23-8-2018 Brandon Gormley frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning defenseman Brandon Gormley feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brandon Gormley was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 285 NHL games during his career, notching 20 goals and adding 50 assists along with 159 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
