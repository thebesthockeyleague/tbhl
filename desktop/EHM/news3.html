17-8-2018 Sean Kuraly frustrated
Boston Bruins center Sean Kuraly feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Sean Kuraly was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 5th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 25 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.
4-8-2018 Peter Cehlarik frustrated
Boston Bruins right wing Peter Cehlarik feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Peter Cehlarik was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
22-7-2018 Boston Bruins trade rumoured
Boston Bruins are rumoured to be planning a trade sending right wing Brian Ferlin and left wing Jesse Gabrielle and BOS 3rd round pick in 2020 draft to San Jose Sharks for right wing Melker Karlsson

Rumours from various sources around the league say that the Boston Bruins are looking for a 1st line right wing and that Melker Karlsson would be a good fit there.
