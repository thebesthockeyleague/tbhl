25-8-2018 Wiley Sherman frustrated
Boston Bruins defenseman Wiley Sherman feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Wiley Sherman was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 5th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
22-8-2018 Boston Bruins trade
Boston Bruins traded David Krejci to Buffalo Sabres for Will Borgen and Conor Sheary

A native of USA, Conor Sheary, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 26 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.

A native of Czech, David Krejci was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 623 NHL games during his career, notching 134 goals and adding 338 assists along with 240 penalty minutes. The 32 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.
